Final Recommendation
Variant A — or an A+C hybrid if you want the bee
Variant A (Floating Pill) is the strongest all-around choice — it covers all 5 modules, uses your teal brand color boldly, has an unmissable active state, and is very straightforward to implement in React Navigation's bottom tab navigator.

If keeping the bee logo matters for brand consistency, use a Variant C approach — place the bee as a golden FAB in the center, and use it to trigger a quick-action sheet (New Booking, Currency Convert, etc.). This way the bee becomes a functional element, not just decoration.
